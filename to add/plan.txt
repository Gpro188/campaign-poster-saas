Implementation Plan - Add Frame Preview and WhatsApp Sharing Form to Campaign Welcome Page
This plan outlines the design and implementation details for enhancing the campaign's supporter landing page (the welcome page/upload step). We will add:

A beautiful Frame Preview showing the campaign poster template using the existing CampaignFramePreview component.
A premium WhatsApp Sharing Widget/Form that allows supporters to easily customize an invite message and share the active campaign link directly on WhatsApp, along with a handy "Copy Link" shortcut.
User Review Required
IMPORTANT

The welcome page (rendered by CampaignClient at step upload) currently shows a single centered upload box. We propose changing this to a modern, highly engaging two-column layout on desktop (stacking gracefully on mobile):

Left Column: The live render of the Campaign's Poster Frame (using the pre-existing CampaignFramePreview component) along with campaign stats (e.g. number of posters generated).
Right Column: The existing Photo Upload dropzone followed by a new WhatsApp Campaign Sharing Form.
This design immediately shows supporters the high-quality template they are about to customize and provides an instant call-to-action to spread the word.
